WHO AM I?

I am a polyvalent freelancer based in Brussels who aims to tackle as many different creative competences possible in order to offer a very wide skillset to any potential client. I have a wide background, having studied both graphic design and carrying a master degree in Fine Arts, attained at Luca school of arts in Brussels as well as 7 years of experience giving individual and group classes of Dutch and English language. I have a huge passion for photography and filmmaking and am a skilled illustrator. This website aims to display this first.

WHAT CAN I DO FOR YOU?

As a designer, I can create many things for you; a corporate identity, a logo, a poster, you name it! As a skilled photographer and videographer, I can shoot your event, wedding, music video,... I can also offer all inlusive packages and take care of the entire creative side of your brand or company.

WHY I DO WHAT I DO?

Because I am obsessed with everything visual, with creating anything that strikes the eye and changes peoples perception. I am currently studying a Ba in Multimedia and creative technologies to further enhance my capabilities. I want to be able to provide my potential clients with an even more complete creative package, being able to develop websites (like this one), motion graphics, interactive experiences etc.

WHERE TO REACH ME?

I am based in the city of Brussels, Belgium but I can go anywhere to fullfill creative duties. You can reach me at jochem.casier@gmail.com with any questions you might have.
